Design Principles Document

Michael Hyer

Alignment

Target

target.com

Target demonstrated alignment by placing items and links next to each other in an eye-pleasing manner.

Proximity

Ebay

ebay.com

Ebay demonstrated proximity by keeping items such as shoes or valentines day gifts in the same areas of viewport.

White Space

Barnes and Noble

barnesandnoble.com

Barnes and Noble demonstrated white space and clean design by not cluttering the screen with too many elements at the same time.
